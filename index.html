In this data, you will find out which limited liability company has the most housing units in Hartford and outside of Connecticut. Before you dive in, this project is focused on helping our community partners, Center for Leadership and Justice, that is established in Connecticut. Their goal is focused around the #NoMoreSlumlords campaign. A housing policy was passed, making LLC owners come forward and not have anymore loop holes for poor housing conditions. This data is to show the viewer which companies own the most living units in Hartford, and also outside of the state of Connecticut. This data will help CLJ because it will show which areas are heavily populated by these LLC owners in Hartford. To view more into where these LLC’s are, and how many units they have, you can click this link.
